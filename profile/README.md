## Selected Work

![OrderLens](image/README/OrderLens.png)

**Retail verification, redesigned for speed and accuracy.**

OrderLens is an AI-powered verification product for POS workflows. It checks whether the tray in front of the cashier actually matches the order in the system, reducing avoidable mistakes at the point of handoff.

Built for fast-paced food service environments, OrderLens combines product flow, computer vision, and review surfaces into one clear operational experience.

**Focus:** tray verification, mismatch detection, cashier workflow, audit visibility  
**Stack:** `Next.js` `React` `TypeScript` `Tailwind CSS` `Vision AI`

![FinovaAI](image/README/FinovaAI.png)

**Document intelligence for modern fintech workflows.**

FinovaAI transforms unstructured documents into structured, review-ready data for loan verification and KYC operations. It is designed for teams that need faster processing, stronger validation, and less manual handling across critical document workflows.

By combining OCR, backend services, and LLM-based extraction, FinovaAI creates a more reliable path from raw files to usable financial data.

**Focus:** OCR, extraction, validation, reviewer workflow, document operations  
**Stack:** `Next.js` `FastAPI` `PostgreSQL` `MinIO` `PaddleOCR` `OpenCV` `LLMs`
